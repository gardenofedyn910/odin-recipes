Homemade Smash Burgers

A very thin patty sandwiched between two buns with various toppings.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Toppings

How to make:

  1. Prepare Patties: Divide 1 1/2 lbs of ground beef into 8 equal parts.
  2. Prep Toppings: Fully prep your toppings ahead of time.
  3. Cook Patties: Place cold balls of meat onto the hot survace. Work quickly and cover each sheet with a piece of parchment paper and smash each into a thin patty then remove, season, and sear before flipping and repeating this step for the other side.
  4. Add Cheese: After flipping and allow to melt.
  5. Toast Buns: Lightly butter the cut side of your buns and toast in the skillet.
