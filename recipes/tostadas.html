Tostadas

A fried tortilla topped with beef and various vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Tostada Shells
  • Ground Beef
  • Refried Beans
  • Toppings

How to make:

  1. Make Taco Meat: Cook the ground beef in a skillet, breaking it up with a spatula. Season the beef with garlic powder, taco seasoning and salt, to taste.
  2. Prep Toppings: Heat refried beans in a skillet on the stove or in the microwave. Prep and chop the rest of your tostada toppings.
  3. Bake Tostada Shells:
    4. Heat oven to 450 degrees before toasting the tostada shells for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Serve: Spread the top with warm refried beans, add ground beef and remaining toppings before serving.
